Boasting a full-service bar, this historic 1907 Mississippi bed and breakfast is 1.5 mi from Columbus city center. Guests can choose their breakfast time and enjoy free in-room WiFi.

In addition to a garden view, each room features a flat-screen cable TV and small seating area with a chair. Extras include towels, linen, and terry cloth bath robe.

Guests can relax on the garden terrace or go hiking along the Columbus Riverwalk 2 mi away. A 24-hour reception and room service are available at this Columbus Jackie O' House Bed and Breakfast as well.

This bed and breakfast is 12 minutes’ drive from Malco Columbus Cinema and 14 mi from Cedar Point Amusement Park. The Columbus Speedway is 23 minutes’ drive away.

This property also has one of the top-rated locations in Columbus! Guests are happier about it compared to other properties in the area.

Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 9.7 for a two-person trip.

This property is also rated for the best value in Columbus! Guests are getting more for their money when compared to other properties in this city.
