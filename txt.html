The Jackie O' House

Experience the beauty of The Jackie O’ House, a boutique bed and breakfast offering the best of both worlds when it comes to modern amenities and convenient location. Located only 1.2 miles from downtown Columbus and Main Street, this truly hidden gem is quaintly tucked away at the end of 9th Street South. Features six beautiful suites each with a private bath, delicious breakfast, and four acres of outdoor space to explore. Functions for every occasion and size, and offering an on-staff event planner.
